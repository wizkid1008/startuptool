Welcome

This assessment evaluates your company across 7 SMEAT dimensions (Customer, People, Operations, Finance, Analytics, Risk, Impact). Follow the 4-step process to get AI-powered insights and strategic recommendations.

The 4-Step Process:
  1. 1 Company Profile — Enter company details and upload documents
  2. 2 AI Research — Claude analyzes your company and auto-scores
  3. 3 Review & Edit — Adjust scores and view analytics
  4. 4 Strategic Analysis — Get AI-powered insights and recommendations

All data is stored locally on your device. No information is sent to external servers except when using the AI Research feature with your explicit permission.
